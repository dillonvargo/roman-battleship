No Ambiguity Test

Hit vs Miss Distinction:

MISS Markers:

  • ○ Circle symbol (hollow)
  • Light blue/cyan color
  • Wave ripple effect
  • Neutral, calm appearance

HIT Markers:

  • ✕ Cross symbol (solid)
  • Red/brown color
  • Blood/damage effect
  • Aggressive, violent appearance

✓ Completely different symbols, colors, and effects ensure zero ambiguity
